About Cafe MAJI

A vibe for every occasion.

Cafe MAJI is more than just a coffee shop; it's a destination. Our super cool atmosphere is perfect for dates, Instagram photos, or simply enjoying a calm moment.

Designed to feel like a chic, high-school party, our space is decked out with countless details that create a unique and memorable experience. Whether you're here to work, catch up with friends, or enjoy a delicious dessert, you'll find a welcoming and friendly environment.
